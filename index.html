CAN I BOOK MY BAND AT JOE'S?

We accept reservations for select game watches and trivia nights only. We do require at least three guests in your party and everyone must be present at least 30 minutes prior to the start of the game or event. Visit our Sports or Trivia pages to make a reservation for an upcoming event.

We provide valet parking for $15 during operating hours. Street parking is extremely limited near Joe's. Pay lots are in the area and their price ranges from $10-$20 depending on the day of the week and length of your stay.

Yes, Joe's allows for guests to enter our restaurant bar before concert doors open and we have our full menu and seating available.

You can purchase tickets on ticketweb.com or by coming into the Joe's box office (located in the main office in the bar, to the left once you've entered the front door).
We accept cash or credit at the box office. There is a $2-3 city amusement tax fee per ticket. A credit card transaction fee applies to all credit transactions.

If you are interested in having your band play at Joe's, please send an email to music@joesbar.com. All submissions are reviewed and if we feel that you are a good fit for Joe's, we will be in touch with you.
