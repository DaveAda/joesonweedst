BOOK YOUR NEXT PRIVATE PARTY AT JOE'S

From Private Events to Party Packages, Joe's on Weed St. is committed to making your event memorable!
Joe's can accommodate events for 10 to 1,200 people in one our six event spaces.
Browse the spaces and Party Packages, below, to find which best fits your upcoming occasion.
Please fill out an inquiry form found next to your preferred room.
For general inquiries, please email Events@joesbar.com and our event coordinators will contact.

The Chapter Room

Upon entering Joe's you will be welcomed into a 3,100 sq. ft. space featuring table seating as well as 6 large comfortable booths, each with 2 built in personal 19 in. flat screens, a private bar with 8 mounted 60-in. flat screen plasmas as well as 6 more 50-in. plasmas mounted throughout the room. This room is a great spot for an upscale event and provides abundant seating. The Chapter Room can comfortably hold 200 people with seating for 150 people.

The Clubhouse

This 8,500 sq. ft. area has a spacious dance floor, a permanent stage for live music, three full bars, five 8 ft. projector screens and over twenty 42 in. flat screen plasmas. The Clubhouse can comfortably hold 800 people for a reception style event as well as seat 250 people for a more formal banquet style event.

V.I.P. Loft Lounge

Overlook the Clubhouse and the stage as you relax in our VIP Loft! Complete with leather lounge furniture, contemporary lighting and granite bar tops, it is the perfect spot for an upbeat upscale party. The lounge features thirteen flat screen plasmas and a private bar. The VIP Loft can comfortably accommodate up to 75 people.

The Rooftop

Built in April of 2013, the Rooftop is the newest addition to Joe's and makes for the perfect place to host your next outdoor event. Located directly above the restaurant, the cozy yet modern 3,500-square-foot space features custom cedar benches and tables perfect for enjoying those coveted Chicago summers. The rooftop can comfortably accommodate 120 guests.
